# SEO Metadata Guidelines

Generate metadata that is accurate, keyword-rich, and readable.

## Rules

- **title**: Descriptive, includes primary keyword, 50-60 chars, no clickbait
- **metaTitle**: Same as title or slight variation, under 60 chars
- **metaDescription**: 150-160 chars, includes primary keyword, summarizes the article clearly
- **slug**: lowercase, hyphen-separated, no special chars
- **tags**: 5-8 relevant tags
- **primaryKeyword**: The main search term the blog targets
- **relatedKeywords**: 3-5 naturally related terms
- **readingTime**: Estimate in minutes (avg 200 words/min)

## Avoid

- Keyword stuffing in title or description
- Generic titles like "Everything You Need to Know About..."
- Misleading or exaggerated claims
- Duplicate tags

## Good Examples

- title: "Climate Change Impact on India: Causes, Effects & Solutions"
- metaDescription: "Explore how climate change is affecting India's environment, health, and economy — and what the government is doing about it."
- slug: "climate-change-impact-on-india"
- tags: ["climate-change", "india", "environment", "upsc", "gs3"]
