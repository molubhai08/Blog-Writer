# UPSC General Studies Papers Reference

## GS-I: Indian Heritage and Culture, History and Geography of the World and Society
- Indian Art & Culture (architecture, paintings, literature)
- Modern Indian History (Freedom struggle, personalities)
- Post-independence India
- World History (18th century onwards)
- Indian Society (social issues, urbanization)
- Geography (physical, human, world geography)

## GS-II: Governance, Constitution, Polity, Social Justice and International Relations
- Indian Constitution & Polity
- Governance & Administration
- Social Justice & Welfare schemes
- International Relations (bilateral/multilateral issues)

## GS-III: Technology, Economic Development, Biodiversity, Environment, Security and Disaster Management
- Indian Economy & Development
- Agriculture & Food security
- Science & Technology
- Environment & Biodiversity
- Internal Security (cyber security, terrorism, border issues)
- Disaster Management

## GS-IV: Ethics, Integrity and Aptitude
- Ethics & Human Values
- Attitude, Emotional Intelligence
- Moral Thinkers & Philosophers
- Public Service Values & Governance
- Case Studies (situation-based questions)
